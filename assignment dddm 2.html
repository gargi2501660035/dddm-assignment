What does PDF compression do? It mainly affects the images inside your PDF. The compression reduces their quality, usually by lowering their DPI value.

How can I reduce PDF file size online? Simply upload your PDF and choose a preset to get the result you want. You can upload files from your computer or phone, from a URL, or from cloud storage services such as Google Drive or Dropbox.

How to reduce PDF file size

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Choose a compression method (basic, strong, preset).
  3. Convert images in the PDF to grayscale to compress the file even more. (optional)
  4. Click "Start" when you are ready.
